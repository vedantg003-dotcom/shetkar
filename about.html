Our Story

Shetkari Krushi Seva Kendra Rahimatpur was established with a vision to serve the farming community of Maharashtra with premium quality agricultural products. Located on Aundh Road in Rahimatpur, Satara district, we have been a trusted partner for thousands of farmers.

Our journey began with a simple mission - to provide genuine agricultural inputs at fair prices while offering expert guidance to help farmers maximize their crop yields. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most respected agricultural stores in the region.

  • Authorized dealer of top agricultural brands
  • Wide range of fertilizers, seeds, and pesticides
  • Expert advice from experienced agriculturalists
  • Competitive prices with bulk order discounts
